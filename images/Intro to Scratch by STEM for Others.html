Intro to Scratch

Dive into our Scratch Programming course and unleash your creativity by designing awesome games and animations from scratch! Perfect for beginners, this course introduces you to the world of coding through fun, visual programming. You'll learn how to create interactive stories, animate characters, build mini-games, and solve real coding challenges. You'll also learn sequencing, loops, and variables in an easy-to-understand way. With step-by-step guidance and plenty of room for imagination, you’ll develop problem-solving skills, boost digital confidence, and have tons of fun bringing your wildest ideas to life!
